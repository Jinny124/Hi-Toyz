Welcome to Our Toy Store!

Find endless fun and excitement at our HI-TOYZ
where imagination comes to play since 2019

In Wijaya established HI-TOYZ. From childhood, Alex loved collecting rare toys as unique
works of art. But Alex has trouble finding the toys he wants to collect, and he has to search
many toy stores to find the toys he wants. It became an idea to build a rare and unique toy store.
Finally, his store was named HI-TOYZ as a symbol of happiness for the collectors. Currently,
Alex has established partnerships with several well-known toy collectors and HI-TOYZ has
become a center of activity for toy collectors. HI-TOYZ will be a reseller of famous toys such as
Hot Wheels, Lego, Gundam and others.
